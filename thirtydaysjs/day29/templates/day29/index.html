Ice Cream History

200 AD

Ancient Chinese records tell of frozen mixture of milk and rice that was consumed as a summer treat.

Late 13th century

Marco Polo returns to Italy, carrying with him the tale and recipe for ice cream. Italy becomes fascinated with findings from Middle East and Asia and starts the age of Renaissance.

1744

Scottish colonists brought ice cream recipe to North America.
